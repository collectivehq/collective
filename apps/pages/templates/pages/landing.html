Everything your group needs

One platform, from first idea to final decision.

Structured discussions

Organise conversations in a tree of topics and sub-topics so nothing gets lost in a flat chat.

Opinions & reactions

Agree, disagree, or abstain on any discussion. React to individual posts with likes and dislikes.

Resolution tracking

Accept, reject, or close discussions to capture outcomes and keep the group moving forward.

Subscriptions & notifications

Subscribe to the topics you care about and get notified when something new happens.

Flexible roles & permissions

Define facilitators, members, and observers with fine-grained permissions tailored to your group.

Import from DOCX & Markdown

Seed a space instantly from a structured document — great for workshops and pre-planned agendas.

How it works

Up and running in minutes.

Step 1

Create a space
